About

Dear Friends and Family,

I was hoping to writing a letter sharing with you how I was well on my way to completing a marathon. I would have shared with you the miles I had run and how close I was to being ready.

Unfortunately, this isn’t that letter. I have had many setbacks and have had to depend on the help of the people closest to me for motivation, training and funding. And now I am asking for your help.

On April 20th, 2015 I will be running the Boston Marathon to raising funds to impact the world through programs such as, the Water for Life Initiative, Humanitarian Aid, Disaster Response, Micro Business Development and, Women and Children Advocacy.

These projects are led by Global Aid Network (GAiN), which is a worldwide humanitarian relief and development organization dedicated to bringing hope and tangible help to the poor and the suffering.

Their mission is to demonstrate the love of God – in word and deed – to hurting and needy people around the world through relief and development projects.

Will you help me with my campaign? Will you share these causes with others? Because with you helping raise funds, we can make an even greater impact than what GAiN could do alone!

Three things you can do right now to help me:

1. Watch me run and cheer me on!

When ever you see me running on Facebook, Twitter or Runtastic click like! I'll get a cheer in my headphones while I’m running!

2. Sign up for my Thunderclap Campaign to share this page on race day.

3. Consider supporting me with 20$, 40$ or 100$.

Water for Life Initiative

Give Water. Give Life.

This is Mike Woodard, one of my alltime heroes, Cycling 4 Water.

According to the World Health Organization, a child dies from a preventable disease associated with lack of clean water every 20 seconds.

But it doesn’t have to be this way! Often, just 80 meters below their feet are aquifers of pure, disease-free drinking water. By providing one deep-water well, we can help to break the cycle of disease and death and transform communities for a lifetime.

GAiN's Story

GAiN is worldwide humanitarian relief and development organization dedicated to bringing hope and tangible help to the poor and the suffering.

Their mission is to demonstrate the love of God, in word and deed, to hurting and needy people around the world through relief and development projects.

From their humble beginnings they have grown to become part of a diverse, global network with offices in Canada, United States, Germany, Australia, Austria, Latvia, South Korea, Holland and Spain. With distribution networks established throughout Africa, Asia, Europe, the Middle East and the Americas, their compassionate reach is truly global in scale.
